Maydiv — Client Lifecycle Flow

1.  Admin logs into the dashboard.
2.  Admin goes to Clients section and clicks "+ Create Client" — fills in company name, contact person, email, and phone.
3.  System automatically sends a Welcome Email to the client with their login credentials.
4.  Client opens the email and logs into the portal using the provided credentials.
5.  On first login, the system asks the client to set a new password.
6.  Client reads the Service Agreement PDF and clicks "Accept & Sign" to digitally sign it.
7.  Admin creates an Invoice and it appears on the client's dashboard.
8.  Client makes the payment externally and uploads the payment screenshot with a Transaction ID.
9.  Admin reviews the payment proof and clicks "Approve".
10. System automatically unlocks the full Project Dashboard for the client.
11. Client can now view the project timeline, assigned team, and live Gantt chart.
12. As each development phase is completed, the team raises a Milestone for client review.
13. Client either clicks "Approve" to proceed or "Request Changes" with remarks.
14. Once all milestones are approved, Admin clicks "Mark as Completed".
15. Client submits a final Star Rating and written feedback. Project lifecycle is complete.
